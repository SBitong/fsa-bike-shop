Mountain-Bikez

"Just a bike store. Nothing more."

A bike shop created online during Pre-pandemic 2020 and started slow. Owner lives in Davao City. Just a small retailing business that focuses more on its Inventory of bikes and aim to build a more bright future for its customers.

This website is created by Stian Trond Bitong. Its a simple monochromatic website that mainly focuses on APIs, javascript, and CSS.

Mountain Bikes that are best-sellers are namely: Stout, Santa Cruz, Specialized, and Giant. Probably the most Budget-Friendly is the Twitter.
